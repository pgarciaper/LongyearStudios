GOING THROUGH THE E-MOTIONS

The nominations for the 90th Academy Awards have been announced, and “The Shape of Water” leads the field with 13 nominations. In Longyear Studios we have analyzed the intensity of the film and  measured the evolution of the concentration of the spectators, giving value to what until now were emotions, thanks to our customized techniques.

BIG DATA PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS SOLUTIONS

Big data analytics can also be used as a predictive tool. In this case, we have used techniques to analyze the IMDB databases to rate the nominated films according to their gender, budget, principals, directors, facebook and twitter likes.... Here is the result
